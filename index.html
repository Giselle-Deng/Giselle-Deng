The Growth of Airbnb in San Francisco

Author:Giselle Deng

In 2008, many hotels around the world were struggling how to survive through the global financial crisis. At the same time, a new San Francisco-based company, Airbnb, was born. This company has changed the travel lodging industry. It leverages the sharing economy as a new way of meeting the needs of visitors and it also drives the econmoic impact of tourism to neighborhoods throughout the city. As of today, Airbnb is prevalent in over 190 countries and has more than 4 million listings worldwide.

The map on the left shows the growth of Airbnb in San Francisco by neighborhood from 2013 to 2017. The average number of airbnb listings in 2013 is 5.81 listings per neighborhood and most listings are located in Fianaical District, Downtown, and South of Market at that time. However, the number of listings in 2014 grows exponentially. There are more than 600 listings in San Francisco, and these listing locations expand to more neighborhoods. As of 2017, the average number of listings in each neihborhood is over 200 and tourists can easily find their favorite airbnb in San Francisco.

The map on the right explores the airbnb listings in 2017 based on different room types. Airbnb hosts can list entrie homes/apartments, private or shared rooms. Generally, the prices for staying in an entire apartment are much more expensive than staying in private or shared room. There are more available entire homes and private room listings than shared room airbnb. However, the price for staying in a private room are not always higher than staying in a shared room. Meanwhile, depending on the number of listings in each neighborhood, the average prices for renting an entire apartment in Financial District are about $481/night. Travellers might consider airbnbs in other neighborhood instead of staying in the Financial District. Surprisely, even though there are a lot of listings in West Addition, the average airbnb price per night in here are still relatively high.

Play around with the menus on both maps. The trending map on the left gives you an insight of how Airbnb has grown in these recent years. By clicking layers of the map on the right , one can see the location of each listing with the corresponding price information.
