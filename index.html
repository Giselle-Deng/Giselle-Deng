The Growth of Airbnb in San Francisco

In 2008, many hotels around the world were struggling how to survive through the global financial crisis. At the same time, a new San Francisco-based company, Airbnb, was born. Airbnb has changed the travel lodging industry. It leverages the sharing economy as a new way of meeting the needs of visitors and driving the econmoic impact of tourism to neighborhoods throughout the city, highlighting local Airbnb hosts who welcome and share their love of the city with vistors. As of today, Airbnb is now prevalent in over 190 countries, where it has 4 million listings worldwide. That's more listings than the top 5 major hotel brands combined.
